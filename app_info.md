# COOKIE — The Digital Gastronome

COOKIE is a high-end, editorial-inspired recipe application designed for the modern home cook who values both aesthetic beauty and culinary precision. It bridges the gap between the tactile charm of a physical heirloom cookbook and the seamless utility of a digital kitchen companion.

## The Vision
The app aims to elevate the act of cooking from a chore to a creative ritual. By focusing on quality over quantity, COOKIE provides a curated space where recipes are treated as stories and pieces of art, rather than just data points.

## Design Philosophy (The Vibe)
The visual identity of COOKIE is built on **intentional pairings** and **sophisticated restraint**:

- **Typography:** A dialogue between the elegant, high-contrast serif **Newsreader** (for headlines and storytelling) and the clean, functional **Work Sans** (for labels and instructions).
- **Color Palette:** A warm, organic foundation of **Off-white (#F5F5F0)**, accented with **Sage Green** and **Burnt Orange** to evoke a sense of nature and the hearth.
- **Layout:** Inspired by premium lifestyle magazines, utilizing generous white space, visible grid structures, and large, immersive imagery.
- **Motion:** Subtle, fluid transitions powered by `motion` that guide the user through the cooking process without being distracting.

## Core Functionality

### 1. The Library
A searchable, filterable grid of curated recipes. Each card highlights the recipe's category, difficulty, and time, with special badges for "Heirloom" recipes.

### 2. Recipe Detail
A deep dive into each dish, featuring:
- **Visual Storytelling:** Large-scale imagery and evocative descriptions.
- **Chef's Notes:** Personal insights and technical tips for a superior result.
- **Ingredient Lists:** Clear, organized lists with visual cues for key items.
- **Preparation Overview:** A high-level look at the steps before diving into the heat.

### 3. Interactive Cooking Mode
A focused, distraction-free interface designed for the kitchen:
- **Step-by-Step Navigation:** Large, legible text that's easy to read from a distance.
- **Progress Tracking:** A persistent progress bar and step counter.
- **Integrated Timers:** One-tap timers for critical steps, keeping the cook on track.
- **Servings Toggle:** Quick access to scaling and serving information.

### 4. Recipe Submission
A space for users to contribute their own heirloom secrets, ensuring the library continues to grow with soul and authenticity.
